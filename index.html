A Charitable Trust that provides Training and Placement Assistance exclusively to undergraduate students in Tier 2 and Tier 3 Colleges, from Extremely Challenged Financial backgrounds

VISION

“To become a one-stop Training and Placement platform for the underprivileged, yet meritorious, students studying in the lesser-known Tier 2 and 3 colleges that are scattered across India's semi-urban and rural areas”

Our Model

How we help

STEP #1

Identify deserving students through entrance tests and personal interviews.

STEP #2

Train extensively on technology tools, aptitude & spoken English.

STEP #3

Provide the right Internships for practical exposure.

STEP #4

Connect the students with companies for Placements.


What we have done thus far


Our Progress



Since Swapnodaya's inception in 2021, the Trust so far upskilled around 40 students, and is currently working with a batch of 110 students for the year 2023-24.



Upskilled students have been successfully placed in companies like Deloitte, Siemens Healthineers, Canopus, Pacewisdom, Thought Process etc.
